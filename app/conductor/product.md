# Initial Concept
StreamCapOrigin is an application designed for capturing, recording, and managing video streams from various platforms, catering to both general users and technical professionals.

# Product Definition

## Target Users
- **General Users:** Individuals seeking a simple and reliable tool to save live video content for offline viewing.
- **Developers and Researchers:** Technical users who require a robust tool for stream data collection, analysis, and extensibility.

## Goals and Values
- **Simplicity and Accessibility:** Providing a user-friendly interface that makes stream recording accessible to everyone, regardless of technical expertise.
- **Reliability and Data Integrity:** Ensuring that recordings are captured with high fidelity and without data loss, even during extended recording sessions.
- **Flexibility and Extensibility:** Supporting a broad range of platforms and providing a foundation that allows for the integration of new features and handlers.

## Key Features
- **Multi-platform Support:** Seamlessly capture streams from popular services such as Twitch, YouTube, and other live-streaming platforms.
- **High-Quality Recording:** Flexible options for resolution and format selection to ensure captures meet specific quality requirements.
- **Scheduled Recording:** Automation capabilities to record streams at specific times or automatically when a creator goes live.

## Performance Requirements
- **Stability:** Consistent and reliable performance over long recording periods and varied network conditions.
- **Scalability:** Optimized to handle multiple concurrent recording tasks without significant performance degradation.
- **Low Latency:** Minimizing the delay between the live broadcast and the local capture to ensure data timeliness.

## Visual Aesthetic and User Experience
- **Modern and Minimalist:** A clean, focused interface that prioritizes recording tasks and reduces visual clutter.
- **Information-Rich and Professional:** Providing deep insights through detailed stream statistics and real-time recording metrics for advanced users.
